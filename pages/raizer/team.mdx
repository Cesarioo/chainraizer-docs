Anyone can become a contributor to the Raizer Platform. Whether you're a developer who can help improve our digital systems, a content creator who can help explain our mission, or a community manager who can help grow our ecosystem - your skills are valuable. 

We believe in fostering an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives come together to build something revolutionary. Contributors can start by joining our Discord community, participating in governance discussions, or submitting pull requests to our GitHub repositories. 

Regular contributors may be eligible for grants and can potentially join the core team as we expand our operations.
